Work at Rooh'd Life

Rooh'd Life is an idea that was set in motion by the vision and commitment of two individuals.

My colleague, Arati Kedia is the co-founder and co-creator of Rooh'd Life and is someone who contributes immeasurably to the development and facilitation of all of its workshops and programmes.

From the beginning we have been united by our goal, viz. to reach out, inform, educate, empower, spread awareness about mental health and psycho-social well-being and offer evidence-based approaches to getting Rooh'd, "the process of becoming calm, joyous and self-actualized."
